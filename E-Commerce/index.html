How to Use the Virtual Dressing Room

Step 1: Create Your Profile

Start by creating a personal profile in the "Manage Persons" section. Upload your photos or use our AR scanning feature to create your virtual model.

Step 2: Browse Collections

Explore our extensive collection of clothing items including shirts, pants, shoes, and complete outfits. Use the navigation menu to switch between different categories.

Step 3: Virtual Try-On

Select any clothing item and use our AR technology to see how it looks on your virtual model. Rotate the view to see the outfit from different angles.

Step 4: Mix and Match

Create complete outfits by combining different pieces. Save your favorite combinations for future reference.

Project Features

Our AR-based virtual dressing system uses advanced computer vision and 3D modeling to provide realistic clothing simulations. Users can see how different outfits look on their virtual avatar in real-time, with accurate fabric physics and lighting.
